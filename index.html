Ad InventoryFraud

Our Vision is to mitigate Ad Inventory Fraud in the Ad-tech Ecosystem by creating awareness about potential fraudulent mechanisms and ultimately make advertising a safe and secure experience for everyone.

Learn moreScroll down

Project


As part of our research, we periodically crawl the problematic websites and associated ad-tech standards (ads.txt & sellers.json) to understand the entities involved in advertising on such websites. Aim is to study the compliance of the ad-tech standards, detect inventory misrepresentation based fraudulent instances, and appropriately inform the affected as well as involved entities in an attempt to reduce the Ad Inventory Fraud in the advertising ecosystem.
